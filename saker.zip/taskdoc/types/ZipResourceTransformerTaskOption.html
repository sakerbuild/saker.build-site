ZipResourceTransformerTaskOption
In bundle: saker.zip
Represents a ZIP resource transformer that should be used when constructing ZIP entries. The transformers should be used with the saker.zip.create() or related tasks. To add transformers to a ZIP creation, consult the use-case documentation of your transformer of interest.
